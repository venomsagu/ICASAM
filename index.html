About BNMIT

B N M Institute of Technology was established in 2001 and is among the top preferred technical institutes in Karnataka. BNMIT was granted Autonomous Status in June 2021 by UGC and VTU. Located in Banashankari, Bengaluru, the lush green campus offers a serene academic environment with easy accessibility to all essential facilities.

BNMIT offers undergraduate programs in Electronics & Communication, Electrical & Electronics, Computer Science, Information Science, Mechanical Engineering, and AI & ML, as well as PG programs in VLSI & Embedded Systems, CSE, and MBA.

The institute is accredited by NAAC with an 'A' Grade and all eligible UG programs are NBA-accredited. BNMIT is recognized by NIRF Innovation (150–300), holds a Diamond Rating by QS I-Gauge, and ranks 26th (TIME Survey), 34th (Outlook), and 40th (DataQuest).

About the Conference

The International Conference on Applied Sciences and Advanced Materials (ICASAM 2025) will unite researchers, academicians, and industry professionals to discuss breakthroughs in applied sciences and material technologies. Hosted by the Departments of Mechanical Engineering and Basic Sciences, ICASAM 2025 will emphasize topics like advanced materials, nanotech, sustainability, and innovation.

The event, scheduled for November 28–29, 2025, will feature keynote speeches, technical sessions, and networking opportunities, fostering global collaboration and innovation.

About Bengaluru

Bengaluru, known as the "Silicon Valley of India", is the capital of Karnataka and a hub for technology, startups, and education. The city blends tradition with modernity and offers a vibrant lifestyle.

  • Climate: Pleasant throughout the year with mild summers and cool winters.
  • Languages: Kannada (official), English, Hindi, and other regional languages are widely spoken.
  • People: Warm, diverse, and culturally inclusive, Bengaluru welcomes individuals from all over India and abroad. Home to over 8 million residents and a metropolitan population exceeding 11 million, Bengaluru ranks as the third most populous city and the fifth-largest urban agglomeration in India.
  • Transportation: Well-connected via BMTC buses, Namma Metro, cabs, autos, and trains. The Kempegowda International Airport connects the city globally.
